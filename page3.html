About Us

Establishment

D-Tech Computers, one of the most growing organization established in 2005 at Badlapur, Thane, Maharashtra, India. Firstly it is established at Badlapur West & now there are two more branches, one is in Badlapur East and seond is in Vangani West.

Our Services

Its a Computer Education Center, providing computer education since from last 15 years. 
Our mission is - rendering quality training at affordable fees. Our uniquely designed curriculum makes our students employable and future ready. We have a dedicated team of skilled teachers and counselors to make sure the regular up-gradation and quality delivery with proper career guidance. Our professional team works harmoniously in unison, pushing the frontiers growth and quality in imparting education.
